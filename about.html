-My Story-

My name is Jordan Adams, currently 20 years old soon to be 21 at the end of this month. I was born in Visalia, Ca and currently still live in Visalia. When I was litte, I moved a lot from different schools since I also lived in Tulare and Woodlake. Once 3rd grade hit, I considered it to be concrete ground because my parents finally bought a house and it was very close to an elementary school, Veva Blunt in which I attended. After finishing school there I went to the normal middle school every kid goes to in the local school district around us. Same for highschool and after I graduated, I was off to College of the Sequoias. I had a few majors in mind, but I was changing them so frequently. I also started to work part time jobs which included Kmart, Lowes, and JcPenney. Most of the jobs that I had were seasonal/temporary and once I bought my own car, I went full time. Working at Cigna as a Contract Rep has been the best job that I have had so far. Although it isn't easy, I still learn new things everyday and when I am off I go straight to my laptop since I take all of my classes online. Being a full time worker and student takes a vast majority of your time, but when I do get a break I like to go fishing, play hockey, travel, watch movies, and most of all spend time with friends and family.
