Photographer

I was never into photography and never liked to be in pictues, but once I took a class on it in highschool, it completely changed my thinking on it. I wasn't to serious about photography until my senior year in highschool. Not really sure on what went on it my mind, but one day I was like, I would like to be a photographer on the side. Seeing an image isn't just a picture, it's a story. I've only done one side job which required me to take pictures of a house that was for sale. I loved it. It wasn't hard, it jsut made me think what angles should I shoot at because these photos were being put up on the brokers website. It was a really fun and interesting time I had shooting for this modern broker and its something that I would like to do as a side job.
