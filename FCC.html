Fresno City College

Fresno City College was never an option for me to attend, but after realizing that it had way more times for different classes that I needed to take and more majors, I decided to enroll in the summer of 2019. I have not taken any classes on campus since I prefer to take online classes plus I live in Visalia. So far I have averaged A's and B's in all the classes that I have taken so far, so that is a good sign. I will be transferring from here, I am just unsure on where I would like to go, although I have 3 colleges in mind. Before I enrolled in the summer, I actually thought about going to this college sooner. I even came to the event where if your new they set you up with everything and talk to a counselor. When I started to look for classes, I just told my best friend that was with me, I think I will just stay at College of the Sequoias. That is what I did, but then I came back and now most of my classes are taken here. I wish I would have attended here earlier since I most likely would have been done faster but I am glad that I am here at this college and can't wait to achieve my Associates.
