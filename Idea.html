Colleges

On this page you will find 3 different colleges that I currently have on my mind for when I am ready to transfer at the end of this year.

University of San Diego

San Diego University is one out of the three colleges that I would like to attend when I am ready to transfer. Not only is it a beautiful school, but the city is filled with lots of fun and the ocean isn't to far either. My best friend also just moved there so it would be cool to live there knowing he isn't to far.

Cal State Monterey Bay

Monterey Bay College is very nice as well and what caught my attention about this school and the city is that I grew up coming here a lot when I was little. From the ocean, to the aquarium and missionaries, the urban living environment that I hear is really nice and relaxing.

University of Nevada, Reno

Nevada State would be my final option as a transfer for me. Actually either Nevada or Washington would be a consideration on whether I transfer out of state. I haven't been to Nevada much, but I have seen a small portion of it and its really beautiful. I also have family there so I hope it wouldn't be so hard if I go. I have just choses a college out of state because I would like to live somewhere else out of California, get to know what its like. I love to travel and I know its a fortune transferring out of state, but so far I am leaning out of state.
